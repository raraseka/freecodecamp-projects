Nasi Goreng

Nasi goreng is an Indonesia food. It's simple to make and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Red Onion
  • Egg
  • Chili
  • Sweet sauce

Instructions

  1. Blend the spices(onion, red onion, and chili) and them stir fry them. Drain the spices
  2. Stir fry the egg and then add the spices
  3. Add the rice and stir fry. Don't forget to add salt and sweet sauce
  4. Done
